My Projects

WANDER POKHARA

Wander Pokhara is an innovative web application that empowers users to uncover and share hidden gems in and around Pokhara. Whether it's a lesser-known trail, a secluded spot, or a cultural site off the beaten path, Wander Pokhara provides detailed information about each destination, including its history, cultural significance, and key attractions. Users can contribute by adding new locations, sharing their personal experiences, and offering insights, making it easier for others to explore and appreciate the rich tapestry of the region.

MOVIE LIST

MovieList is a sleek and user-friendly website built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, designed to help users create and manage their personalized movie watchlists. The site allows users to search for movies, add them to their list, and organize them by categories or genres, all within a clean and intuitive interface.
